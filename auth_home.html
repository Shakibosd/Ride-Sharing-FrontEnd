Ride Sharing


Ride-sharing is a modern transportation model that allows individuals to share a vehicle for a journey, usually coordinated through
a digital platform or mobile app. It provides an alternative
to traditional taxis and public transportation, offering a convenient,
cost-effective, and environmentally friendly option for commuters.
